PoC: Synchronized High speed data acquisition


Aim:
Test the synchronization of high-speed Oscilloscope and high-speed Transceiver


Introduction:
Test the synchronization of high-speed Oscilloscope (PXIe-5105) and high-speed Transceiver(NI-5783) in 400MS/s

Method:
The LabVIEW and LabVIEW FPGA programs were developed to customize the test program and evaluate the synchronization results. The program utilized the NI-5783 to output random wave data at a rate of 400MS/s. The sampling clock from the NI-5783 was shared with itself and the PXIe-5105 through the PXI bus. Both the NI-5783 and PXIe-5105 sampled the data using the same clock set at 60MHz or a lower frequency.

The LabVIEW FPGA program was configured to generate random wave data at a rate of 400MS/s using the NI-5783. Synchronization between the NI-5783 and PXIe-5105 was established by sharing the sampling clock via the PXI bus. The sampling functionality was implemented for both the NI-5783 and PXIe-5105, utilizing the shared clock at a frequency of 60MHz or lower.

The LabVIEW program included a user interface for controlling and monitoring the test program. The LabVIEW FPGA program was integrated within the LabVIEW environment. The acquired data from both the NI-5783 and PXIe-5105 were displayed for synchronization analysis. The sampling clock frequency could be adjusted as required (60MHz or lower).

By executing the LabVIEW and LabVIEW FPGA programs, the customized test program allowed for the evaluation of synchronization results between the NI-5783 and PXIe-5105. The LabVIEW interface facilitated control, monitoring, and analysis of the acquired data, enabling an assessment of the synchronization effectiveness at the desired clock frequency.
