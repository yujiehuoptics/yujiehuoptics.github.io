Application and Correction of Distortion in Fourier Domain Optical Coherence Tomography (FD-OCT)


Description:
Research on measurement of refractive index of the sample
Time: Oct.2014-Oct.2015

Introduction:
Through analyzing the distortion of OCT images, I measured the refractive index of the sample. Furthermore, based on the cause of distortion, I wrote a program to correct the distortion. For high speed image acquiring, I also wrote a LabVIEW program to control all the electrical and movement part including PZT (PI), CCD (Point Grey) and Scanning mirror (Thorlabs)



Layour of cover glass, raw data & corrected data


Data acquistion and process software
